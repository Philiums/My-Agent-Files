# Ritual — Daily Practice

*The daily rhythm that keeps Philippe sharp and moving forward.*

---

## ☀️ Morning Briefing (Upon Waking)

**Purpose:** Orient for the day ahead

1. **Weather Check** — Will I need to leave the house? What to wear?
2. **Overnight Market Recap** — Crypto moves, futures, Asia/Europe sessions
3. **News Scan** — AI developments, crypto regulation, macro headlines
4. **Calendar Review** — Any meetings, deadlines, or time-sensitive tasks?
5. **Priority Focus** — What's the ONE thing that matters today?

---

## 📊 Pre-Market Prep (9:00 AM EST)

**Purpose:** Trading mindset and positioning

1. **Portfolio Snapshot** — TFSA, FHSA, margin, crypto positions
2. **Overnight Alerts** — Any positions hit targets or stop-losses?
3. **Market Sentiment** — VIX, fear/greed, social chatter
4. **Watchlist Review** — Levels to watch, entry/exit plans
5. **Trade Journal** — Log yesterday's moves if not done

---

## 🧹 Midday Maintenance (12:00 PM)

**Purpose:** Keep systems clean

1. **File Organization** — Downloads, screenshots, workspace cleanup
2. **Git Sync** — Commit and push any changes
3. **Memory Update** — Log morning developments to daily file
4. **Agent Library Check** — Check heyron-ai/agent-library for new skills/resources
5. **Inbox Triage** — Quick scan, flag urgent, archive noise

---

## 🌆 Evening Wind-Down (6:00 PM)

**Purpose:** Close the day intentionally

1. **Market Close Recap** — How did positions perform? Any news?
2. **Trade Review** — What worked? What didn't? Vibe vs analysis check
3. **Tomorrow's Setup** — Pre-market watchlist, key levels
4. **Daily Log** — Capture decisions, insights, lessons to MEMORY.md
5. **Gratitude/Reflection** — One win, one lesson, one thing to carry forward

---

## 🌙 Optional: Night Owl Mode (10:00 PM)

**Purpose:** Deep work or esoteric exploration

1. **Prediction Market Check** — Kalshi/Polymarket opportunities
2. **Research Deep-Dive** — New strategies, tools, or concepts
3. **Reading List** — Articles, docs, or books in queue
4. **Creative Mode** — Business ideas, content, or side projects

---

## 🔄 Weekly Rituals (Sundays)

1. **Week Review** — What moved the needle? What drained energy?
2. **Portfolio Analysis** — Performance by account, strategy effectiveness
3. **Goal Check-In** — Progress on wealth generation, organizational skills
4. **Ritual Audit** — Is this routine still serving me? What needs adjusting?
5. **Week Ahead** — Big picture priorities, potential market events

---

## ⚡ Trigger Phrases

- **"Run my morning ritual"** — Full morning briefing
- **"Pre-market prep"** — Trading focus mode
- **"Close the day"** — Evening wind-down
- **"Ritual check"** — Quick status on today's practice

---

*Rituals create consistency. Consistency creates results.*
